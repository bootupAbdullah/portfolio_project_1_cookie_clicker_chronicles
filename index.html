How to Play Cookie Clicker Chronicles

Welcome to Cookie Clicker Chronicles! Here's how to play:

  • Click on each quadrant of the cookie to make it disappear
  • Complete all 4 quadrants to eat one whole cookie
  • Track your total clicks and cookies eaten on the scoreboard
  • Special messages appear as you reach click milestones!
